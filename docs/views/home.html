About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at TU Berlin, Germany, under the supervision of Prof. Fatma Deniz. I recently defended my doctoral thesis at Inria, France, under the supervision of Dr. Xavier Hinaut and Prof. Alexandre Frederic. I am also a visiting scholar at MaxPlanck Software Systems, Germany, under the supervision of Dr. Mariya Toneva. Previously, I was a Master student at IIIT-Hyderabad, India.

My research interests include:

  • Computational Cognitive Neuroscience
  • Bridging AI & Neuroscience (Neuro-AI)
  • Language Analysis in the Brain and Large Language Models
  • Brain Encoding & Decoding (fMRI, MEG, EEG)
  • Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, and Speech Signal Processing
  • Deep Learning
  • Download CV

  • I gave an Oral Presentation about our Deep Diacritization paper at COLING'2020 (Online).
