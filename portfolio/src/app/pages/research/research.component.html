Research Overview

My research focuses on bridging Artificial Intelligence and Neuroscience, with a particular emphasis on understanding language processing in both brains and machines.

Computational Cognitive Neuroscience

Investigating neural mechanisms underlying cognitive processes using computational models.

Neuro-AI Bridge

Developing methods to align neural networks with brain processing patterns.

Brain Encoding & Decoding

Building models to predict and interpret brain activity patterns (fMRI, MEG, EEG).

Current Projects

Language Models as Human-like Annotators

Investigating how large language models can serve as cognitive models for human language processing.

Multi-modal Brain Encoding
