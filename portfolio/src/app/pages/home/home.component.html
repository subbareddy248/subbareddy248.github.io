About

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at TU Berlin, Germany, under the supervision of Prof. Fatma Deniz. I recently defended my doctoral thesis at Inria, France, under the supervision of Dr. Xavier Hinaut and Prof. Alexandre Frederic. I am also a visiting scholar at MaxPlanck Software Systems, Germany, under the supervision of Dr. Mariya Toneva.

Research

My research interests include:

  • Computational Cognitive Neuroscience
  • Bridging AI & Neuroscience (Neuro-AI)
  • Language Analysis in the Brain and Large Language Models
  • Brain Encoding & Decoding (fMRI, MEG, EEG)
  • Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, and Speech Signal Processing
  • Deep Learning
